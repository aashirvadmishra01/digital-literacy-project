# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

DO's
1. Keep your profile professional – future employers will search your name
2. Think before you post – once online, it can be permanent
3. Use strong, unique passwords on all social accounts
4. Report abusive or suspicious content or accounts
5. Keep your privacy settings updated and reviewed regularly

DON'Ts
1. Don't share your location, phone number, or address publicly
2. Don't post confidential college or exam related information
3. Don't engage with or spread unverified news or rumours
4. Don't accept friend requests from strangers
5. Don't post anything that could embarrass you professionally later
